Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Lead Recovery Systems (“we”, “us”) provides missed call recovery and demo services for service businesses. This policy explains how we collect and use personal information on this website and through our demo phone line.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business name and basic business details
  • Content of messages or calls you send to us
  • Technical metadata (time of contact, IP address, user agent)

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Provide demos of our missed call recovery system
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Schedule calls or follow‑up conversations
  • Improve and troubleshoot our services

SMS Messaging

If you call, text, or submit your phone number through this website, we may send SMS messages related to your inquiry, such as demo details, support, and scheduling information. Message frequency varies based on your interactions. Message & data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP to a message from us. To get help, reply HELP or contact us at josh@leadrecoverysystems.com.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with service providers we use to operate our services, such as Twilio (telephony) and Zapier (automation), strictly for the purpose of delivering the services you request.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
