Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Lead Recovery Systems (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides missed‑call recovery services for service businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information related to our service.

Information we collect

When you contact a participating business using a phone number powered by our service, we may process:

  • Your phone number.
  • Message content you send or receive.
  • Basic call metadata (time, duration, and status of the call).

How we use information

We use this information solely to:

  • Deliver missed‑call recovery SMS messages related to your service request.
  • Help the business respond to your inquiry, schedule visits, and send updates.
  • Maintain and improve the reliability of our service.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • The business you contacted, so they can respond to your request.
  • Trusted service providers (such as telephony or hosting providers) who help us deliver SMS and operate our service.

SMS opt‑out

You can stop receiving SMS messages from our service at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive. You may reply HELP for help or contact the business directly.

Data retention

We retain SMS logs and related data only as long as necessary to provide the service to the business you contacted and to comply with legal and accounting obligations.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: info@leadrecoverysystems.com.
